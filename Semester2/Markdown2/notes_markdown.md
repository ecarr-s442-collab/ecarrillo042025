# Emergency Police Response - Public Service

## Step 1: Emergency call & Dispatch

- Receive call from Dispatch and locate where the situation is at.

## Step 2: Arrival & Scene Security

- Upon arrival, secure the scene of any dangers.

## Step 3: Command & Coordination

- Once Secured, locate the problem and coordinate your team to find solution

## Step 4: Response Actions

- Once Coordinated and found the problem, act on the solution and finish the job

## Step 5: Investigation & Documentation

- Finally once situation is over, investigate what happened and document overview of what happened.
